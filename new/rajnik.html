Top 5 Rajnikanth movies to watch now

In terms of South Indian cinema and the Indian film industry as a whole, Rajinikanth is without a doubt the largest living superstar. He has received several awards, and over the years, he has elevated his stature in Tamil Nadu to that of a deity. He has one of the most fascinating filmographies in India, ranging from odd characters with peculiar speech to leading in films with the biggest budget.

1. Aarilirunthu Aruvathu Varai

2.. Adisaya Piravi

When Kaalayan, who was murdered by thugs, reached paradise and dissented while inhabiting Ballu's body and interacting with his rapacious relatives, Ballu immediately sent him back. Now, Kaalayan must make changes to both his own and Balu's lives.

3. Muthu

Raja, a zamindar, and Muthu, his charioteer, fall in love with Ranganayaki. Unbeknownst to the former, Ranganayaki has a special bond with Muthu.

4.Thalapathi

Thalapathi, a contemporary interpretation of the relationship between the two Mahabharata heroes, Karna and the Duryodhana, was hailed as both a critical and financial triumph throughout its theatrical run. Surya, who was abandoned by his mother when he was a toddler, develops into a good young man. He meets Devaraja, a violent but morally honest don, after being cruelly tortured by the police for no apparent reason, and the two become close friends.

5. Mullum Malarum

This film, which is sometimes referred to be Rajnikanth's breakout performance, centres on the orphaned Kali, his sister Valli, and the recently appointed engineer at the power plant that the former works at, Kumaran, with whom Kali has conflict.
