Driving Licence Apply Online

The digitization process is being implemented at a rapid speed under the central government's digital Abhiyan. As a result, the government has established a separate webpage for the registration of driving licences. With the aid of this website, you can get all of the key information about a driving licence, such as about the driving licence, the application procedure for a driving licence, the goal, advantages, features, eligibility requirements, required papers, and so on. So, if you want to receive all of the information about a driving licence, you need read this article all the way through.

How To Apply for Driving License Online

  1. NavigatNavigate to parivahan.gov.in
  2. Select the online services option and then the Driving Licence Related Services option.
  3. Select the state where you wish to apply.
  4. Then, select the "Apply for Driving Licence" option.
  5. Fill out the form completely.
  6. Scanned copies of the relevant papers should be uploaded.
  7. Make the application fee payment.
  8. Make an appointment for a driving test.
  9. Go ahead and take the test on time.

How To Apply Offline for DL?

  1. You must get Form 4, which is the application form for a driving licence in India.
  2. The Form is available for download from the state transportation official webpage.
  3. You may also fill out Form 4 at your local RTO.
  4. Fill out the application form completely and send it to the local RTO together with additional papers such as proof of age and address.
  5. Submit the application fee in person.
  6. You should be required to schedule a driving test.
  7. Attend the exam at the RTO Office.
  8. If you pass the exam, you will receive your driver's licence.
